Sámi soga lávlla ( 'Song of the Sami People', lit. 'Song of the Sámi Sámi Sámi Sámi um uh I mean well um um um so so Sámi Sámi Sámi Sámi Sámi Sámi Family' ) was originally a poem written like so okay well okay well um like okay by Isak Saba that was published in the newspaper Sagai Muittalægje for the first time on April 1, 1906.
