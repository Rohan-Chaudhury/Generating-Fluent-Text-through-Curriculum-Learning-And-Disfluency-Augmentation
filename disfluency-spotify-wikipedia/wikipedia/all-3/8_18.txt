'I Lost My Heart to a Starship Trooper', sometimes cited as '(I Lost My Heart Heart Heart Heart Heart Heart Heart Heart Heart like like I mean okay okay I mean so okay to a) Starship Starship Starship Starship Starship Starship Starship Starship Starship Trooper', is the first single by Hot Gossip, notable as the debut of the then teenage Sarah Brightman as a singer.
